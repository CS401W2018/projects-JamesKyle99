Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) section of our Client Services page. Here, you will find answers to some of the most common inquiries regarding the services we offer. These FAQs are designed to provide quick solutions to help you make the most of our department’s offerings. We encourage you to review them as they cover many topics that can save you time and enhance your experience with us.

If you have any additional questions or if there's something specific you would like to know that isn't covered here, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our team. We are happy to assist you further and ensure all your needs are met.

Question 1. How can I reset my password?
Answer To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
Question 2. How do I request IT support for my computer?
Answer You can request IT support by submitting a service ticket via the IT support portal or by giving us a call at our call center 56730
Question 3. What should I do if my computer is running slow?
Answer If your computer is running slow, try restarting it, closing unnecessary programs, and checking for any updates. If the issue persists, submit a support ticket for further assistance.
Question 4. How can I access the campus Wi-fi?
Answer To access the campus Wifi, you can either connect to BYUH Guest for free or connect to a more secure wifi through BYUH secure with your netID and password to connect.
Question 5. How do I back up my files?
Answer You can back up your files using the cloud storage service provided by the company. Please refer to the backup guide available on the IT portal for step-by-step instructions.
