Our Services

Our department of IT Operations offers a variety of services to ensure your systems run smoothly and efficiently. Below are the core services we provide:

  • Installation of Adobe Applications: We install and configure Adobe software, such as Adobe Acrobat and Adobe Creative Suite, on computers to meet user needs.
  • Printer Installation: We set up and connect printers to computers, ensuring proper configuration and connectivity.
  • Troubleshooting Software Problems: We diagnose and resolve software-related issues, helping you get back to work without interruptions.
  • Adding Users to Computers: We assist with user account creation and management, ensuring that authorized personnel have appropriate access to their systems.
  • Hardware Replacement and Ordering: We manage the replacement and procurement of hardware components to keep your equipment up to date and functional.
