Marvel Cinematic Universe

MCU Hero

Ever since I was a kid, I have always admired super heroes in comic books and cartoons. The Marvel Cinematic Universe are cinematic adaptations of iconic comic book characters like Spiderman, Captain America, Black Panther, The Hulk and many more. It was every child's dream to see these characters come to life, and to see each character interact with each other with great storylines made it better.

What I love about this universe is its not only about fictional superhero movies but each character teaches all of us valuable lessons in life. Al though fictional, they relate the lives of these superheroes to real life situations we encounter in everyday life, like responsibilites, family matters, friendship, and even anxiety and depression that a lot of kids these days experience. Factors like these, are what makes me a fan of this universe.
