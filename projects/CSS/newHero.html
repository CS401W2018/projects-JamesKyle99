My Hero - Spiderman

Interests Hero

Spiderman or also known as Peter Parker, is one of my all time favorite fictional superhero ever since I was a kid. His story arc is what I admired the most. He was just a teenager when his life changed forever when he was bitten by a radioactive spider. At a young age, he learned the importance of sacrifice and responsibilites. One of the most famous quotes from that movie states " With great power, comes great responsibility". This not only applies to his life, but to all of us as well.
